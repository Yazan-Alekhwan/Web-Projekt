horizon zero dawn


Aloy was found all alone in the primal mother, our sacred mountain.Rost, an outcast from the Nora tribe, raised her and taught her the skills of survival. Avoided by the other children and referred to as "non-mother", one day she found herself in a cave built long ago by the ancients. There she encountered a Focus, a device that shows her visions only she can see. Through these visions, Aloy learned more about our world and its past than ten generations of our elders could teach us. With more questions than answers on her mind, she trained with Rost to perfect her hunting and fighting skills so she could face the test known as The Trial. If she passed, she would be accepted as a worthy member of the Nora tribe and perhaps receive some answers to the mysteries.

The last of us


Five years after their perilous journey through a post-apocalyptic United States, Ellie and Joel have settled in Jackson, Wyoming. Here they now live in peace and harmony amidst a vibrant community of survivors - despite the ever-present threat of the infected and other desperate survivors from outside. However, when a violent incident destroys the peaceful coexistence, Ellie embarks on a merciless and brutal hunt for justice. Each and every guilty person should pay with their life - even if it means physically and mentally destructive consequences for them.
